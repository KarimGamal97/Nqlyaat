How to use
A load board is an online platform that connects truckers with
shippers who need to move freight. It is a convenient and
efficient way to find loads that suit your needs and preferences.
  • Sign up for an account
  • Search for loads by entering your location, destination, equipment type, date range, and other criteria.
  • Browse the available loads and check the details, such as shipper name, contact information, payment terms, and load description.
  • Contact the shipper directly or through the load board website to negotiate and confirm the load. Make sure to get a confirmation number and a rate confirmation document before picking up the load.
  • Post your truck on the load board to let shippers know that you are available and looking for loads. You can specify your location, destination
  • Enjoy the benefits of using a load board, such as finding loads faster, saving time and money, expanding your network, and growing your business.
